Start Normal is the alternative to distance learning.

Start Normal began as a group of thousands of parents advocating for normal start to the school year. However, with that looking increasingly unlikely we have begun creating our own network of parents and teachers seeking to connect for full-time, in-person instruction.

There is no cost, no obligation, no politics — we're only looking to educate our kids so they don't fall behind.
