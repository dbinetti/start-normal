Start Normal!

Parents are concerned that the draft reopening framework does not put children or teachers first. It is a public health document that does not address key questions such as:

  • How will masks on children be enforced for true safety?
  • How will teachers educate both in-person and distance learning at the same time?
  • How will the social-emotional needs of students be addressed when staff is being cut?
  • How will basic operations like eating, restroom breaks, and recess be addressed?
  • How long will all this last, really?
  • What are the true costs of this policy to our children's educational, physical, mental, and social-emotional well-being?
  • Are these costs worth the benefit — and how can we parents know if we aren't given the data nor an explantion from the public health officer?

We are calling for the primary driver of this document, San Mateo Public Health Officer Dr. Scott Morrow, to provide the data employed to reach his conclusions. If you agree we are owed an explanation with evidence, please sign our letter.
