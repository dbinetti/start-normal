Start Normal!

Parents are concerned that the draft reopening framework does not strike the proper balance. It is a public health document that does not answer key questions such as:

  • How will teachers perform both in-person and distance-learning at the same time?
  • How will masks on children be enforced for true safety?
  • How do students learn or parents work under a 'hybrid' schedule?
  • How will basic operations like lunch, restroom breaks, and recess be addressed?
  • How will this policy equitably address the needs of the most vulnerable?
  • How much do we improve the safety of students, staff and teachers?
  • What are the true costs of this policy to our children's educational, physical, mental, and social-emotional well-being?
  • Are these costs worth the benefit — and how can we parents know if we aren't given the data?
  • How long will all this last?

We are calling for the primary driver of this document, San Mateo Public Health Officer Dr. Scott Morrow, to provide the data employed to reach his conclusions. If you agree we are owed an explanation with evidence, please sign our letter.
