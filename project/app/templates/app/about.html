Start Normal began as a group of parents advocating for a normal start to the school year in San Mateo County, California. Once it became clear that wasn't going to happen, we decided to help ourselves and formed a network of parents and teachers to support in-person at-home instruction for small, low-risk groups.

We understand that the risks from Covid-19 are real, but believe they are outweighed by our children's educational and social-emotional needs. For those seeking to supplement distance learning, Start Normal provides a solution that puts kids first.
