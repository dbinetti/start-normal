Who I Am and Why I Did This

My name is David Binetti, I'm a dad, and I started this effort because I'm worried about my kids and community.

I’m an entrepreneur who founded companies to help people get easy access to information. I created USA.gov which won the Harvard’s Innovations in American Government award and was named USA Today’s Site of the Year way back in 2000. And I’m a registered independent. My focus is on my two daughters, in second and fifth grade, not politics.

These days I help Fortune 500 companies avoid billion-dollar disasters when faced with high uncertainty. I do this with one simple approach: I get them to test their assumptions before they commit to projects. It seems like such a small, obvious thing, but you’d be amazed how often brilliant, powerful people make tremendously costly mistakes simply because they don’t bother to check something before they act.
