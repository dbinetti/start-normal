My name is David Binetti, I'm a dad, and I started this effort because I'm worried about my kids and community. I volunteer as a crossing guard, help manage the PTA website, donate thousands to our local educational foundation, and attend school board meetings regularly.

I am not political. I'm a registered independent and I've only donated to one politician in my life: former San Mateo County Supervisor Mary Griffin, and the only reason I did that was because she was my kindergarten teacher at Skyline Elementary in Daly City. You won't find me on social media because I quit Facebook in 2014.

I’m an entrepreneur who founded companies to help people get easy access to information. These days I help Fortune 500 companies avoid billion-dollar disasters when faced with high uncertainty. I do this with one simple approach: I get them to test their assumptions before they commit to projects. It seems like such a small, obvious thing, but you’d be amazed how often brilliant, powerful people make tremendously costly mistakes simply because they don’t check their data.

And that's what I'm seeing happening now with the schools, so I decided to do something about it.
