Start Normal originally began as a group of parents advocating for a normal start to the school year in San Mateo County, California. Once it became clear that wasn't going to happen, we decided to help ourselves.

We're building a network of parents and teachers to support in-person at-home instruction for small, low-risk groups.

We understand that the risks from Covid-19 are real but are outweighed by our children's educational and social-emotional needs. For those seeking to supplement or replace distance learning, Start Normal allows parents and teachers to decide for themselves what is in the best interest of our children.
