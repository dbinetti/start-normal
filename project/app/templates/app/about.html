Start Normal originally began as a group of parents advocating for a normal start to the school year in San Mateo County, California. Once it became clear that our politicians had other plans for our kids, we decided to help ourselves.

We're building a network of parents and teachers to support in-home, in-person instruction for small groups of students. We're connecting kids who want to learn with teachers who want to teach — not through a screen, but face-to-face — to create as close to a normal school environment as we can safely accomplish with low-risk groups.

We've seen what works around the world and value science and data over politics. This approach isn't for everyone, but for those who believe the risks of a sub-standard education are greater than the risk of Covid-19 to our children's lives, Start Normal provides an alternative that allows parents and teachers to decide for themselves what is in the best interest of our children.
