Choose your Homeroom

A Homeroom is a pod of students to be taught together as a group. If you already know who you want in your Homeroom, choose "Create Homeroom". Otherwise, choose "Join Homeroom".

If you have multiple students that you want in the same Homeroom, then create a Homeroom for the first child, and then join the Homeroom you just created for your other students.
