Last Step! Build your Homeroom

A Homeroom is a stable pod of students to be taught as a group. Typical Homerooms are about 4-6 students of the same grade with similar preferences for schedule, frequency and safety.

There are two ways to build up your Homeroom:

  • 1.  If you have already formed your own pod you can just add names directly, using the Add Student link below.
  • 2.  You can invite a friend using the homeroom link — just copy & paste and send it in email or text along with a personal message.

We'll start looking for matching teachers right away, but keep in mind most instructors want to see at least three students in your Homeroom to start. We'll send notices when there's a match, and in the meantime you can always update any information from your Dashboard.
